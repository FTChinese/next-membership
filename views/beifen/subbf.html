What is the ePaper and how can I access it?

The FT ePaper is an exact digital replica of the printed newspaper - available from 6am GMT on your laptop, desktop and now also available as a web app, optimised for iPhone, iPad and Android devices. The ePaper is exclusively available to all newspaper and premium digital subscribers.
